Best Paper Award

SASI-ITE'24 recognizes the excellence in research through the best paper award from each Track on the last day of the conference. All accepted papers will be considered for this award. The awards will be announced on the last day of the conference.

The Technical Program Committe will select the Best Paper Award by judging the submission based on the following criterion:

1. Novelty
2. Advancement of the theory and application
3. Quality of the presented results
4. Quality of the written manuscript
5. Practicality and industrial relevance of the presented work
6. Quality of the oral/poster presentation

Travel Grants

We offer limited travel grants to the student participants for attending conference. We will offer travel grants for lumpsum amount to the eligible and selected candidates under this travel Grants. Form for the same will be opened in February, 2024.

For the application, the following information is required::

1. Applying Candidate should be IEEE Member
2. Applying Candidate should be a student (PhD, graduate, or undergraduate)
3. Applying Candidate should be the first author of an accepted paper in the conference.
4. Applying Candidate need to attach the registration payment slip.
5. Only Early bird registration are eligible for this grant.

Regsitration Grants

We offer limited registration grants to the student participants. We will reimburse the registered amount under this registration Grants. The form will be opened in February, 2024.

For the application, the following information is required::

1. Applying Candidate should be IEEE Member
2. Applying Candidate should be a student (PhD, graduate, or undergraduate)
3. Applying Candidate should be the first author of an accepted paper in the conference.
4. Applying Candidate need to attach the registration payment slip.
5. Only Early bird registration are eligible for this grant.
6. A Letter from your supervisor or Institute that you are not getting any financial support for the registration is required for the application. Upload this letter in pdf format.
