Registration Instructions

After you have registered and paid in full for the conference, please follow the instructions below to prepare your final paper and upload it. As a reminder, one (1) registration can be used to upload up to two (2) final papers on which you are an author and if both papers were provisionally accepted.

  1. All authors who have been accepted must register for the conference with non-returnable payment and attend the conference to present their papers in physical mode.

  2. One of the authors should be identified as a presenter for each paper, and his details should be shared via google form.

  3. All the presenters informed to carry any identity proof and it will be verified during the conference registration desk.

  4. Authors who do not attend the conference in physical mode to present their papers after registration, their paper will not be forwarded to IEEE Xplore for publication and the registration amount will not be returned.

  5. Foreign authors are requested to attend the conference in physical mode, but the authors who will not be allowed to travel to the conference due to visa issues or strict national rules will be given the opportunity to present their accepted papers through a virtual presentation on request to the conference chair.

  6. IEEE reserves the right to exclude a submission from distribution after the conference, including exclusion from IEEE Xplore if the submission does not meet IEEE standards for scope and/or quality. Articles excluded from further distribution shall be archived by IEEE but shall not be indexed or appear on IEEE Xplore.
Note:
A registration confirmation code can be associated only to paper(s) in which the registrant is an author or co-author. Violation of this rule, such as associating a registration code with a paper not authored by the registrant, is considered a serious offense.

Accepted Papers

Step 1. Register for conference – Registration is open now.

You MUST register before uploading your final paper. Papers will be included in the proceedings only if at least one author has registered and paid the registration fee. One author registration will cover the publication expenses of two (2) accepted papers. In the case no registration has been received and correctly processed to cover the paper publication, the conference organizers will contact the authors before the paper is removed from the proceedings. No paper will be published without the author’s completed registration and payment.

Step 2. Generate an IEEE Xplore compatible manuscript using PDF Xpress after all the comments given by the reviewers were incorporated. All final manuscripts must be generated using the PDF Xpress tool.
