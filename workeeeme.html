WORKSHOP

Pre Conference Technical Programs of SASI-ITE'24

One Day Workshop on

Electric Vehicle Technology

The workshop is serving to be a great platform for the participants obtain deep insights of Electric Vehicle Technologies, Battery Management Systems which in turn helps them to apply in real time applications.

Objective of Tutorial
• Understand the basics of electric vehicles, their architecture, and fundamentals.
• Power Converters applications in Electric Vehicles.
• Advanced drives and control techniques for EVs.
• Battery managements systems for EVs.
• Electric vehicle market and policies.

Eligibility
PG / UG Students of EEE and Mechanical Engineering
