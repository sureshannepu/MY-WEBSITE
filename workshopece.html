WORKSHOPS

Pre Conference Technical Programs of SASI-ITE'24

A two days workshop on

Ethical Hacking

The workshop is serving to be a great platform for the participants obtains deep insights of Ethical hacking in cyber security which in turn helps them to apply in real time applications



Objective of this workshop
(1) Understand the basics of ethical hacking.
(2) An advanced concept of Linux commends.
(3) Learns the concepts of Bash shell scripting.
(4) Network and Website operation basics
(5) Challenges in hacking process.

Outcomes of this workshop
The participants will be able know the fundamentals of Linux basics for cyber security and concepts of ethical hacking.

Who can attend
UG / PG Students of ECE, ECT, CSE, CST, CSM, IT and other cyber security based branches
